# Overview

## Purpose
This project is a backend API for managing the operations of a small toy retail store.
It focuses on product management, sales processing, stock control, and basic customer data handling.

## Business Context
The system is designed for a physical toy store with a cashier-based sales process.
Sales are handled in real time at the point of sale, and inventory accuracy is critical.

## Target Users
Store cashiers

Store owner / administrator

(No public customer access)

## Scope
Product and inventory management

Sales lifecycle management (draft → confirmed → paid → cancelled)

Stock updates only after payment confirmation

Basic customer data registration

Sales history tracking

## Out of scopre
Online payments

E-commerce storefront

Accounting or tax reporting

Advanced analytics

Supplier management
